3Decision Tree

For every bank deposit, CASEY walks this path.

Each deposit runs through the same sequence. Internal transfer? Skip. Kicker? Route to house. Known carrier? Match to DBCD. Non-Ivans? Search Deal Log. Unknown? Flag.

Input
A single bank deposit lands
Q1 · SkipInternal transfer?
Is this money moving between Safeshield's own accounts (6900 → 8026 sweep)? Not new revenue. Log and move on.
RESULTSkipped from commission total · logged in audit trail
Q2 · HouseKicker or bonus?
Satellite Agency Q4 bonus, carrier profit share, contingent revenue. No producer split. Routes to house income, not payout.
RESULT100% house · zero to producers · recognized as agency income
Q3 · AutoIvans DBCD match?
Does a carrier statement for this amount exist for this payor? Within $1 is exact. Within $500 is variance-flagged but matched.
RESULTMatched to Ivans rows · producer via Deal Log · split computed · done
Q4 · Smart MatchNon-Ivans · Deal Log candidate?
For carriers without DBCDs, CASEY searches Deal Log for recent placements matching carrier, expected amount, and date window. Scores and proposes.
RESULTHigh confidence = 1-click confirm · medium = show options · low = manual
Q5 · BlockNo explanation found?
Unknown payor, no DBCD, no Deal Log candidates. CASEY flags it and blocks close until resolved. Austin uploads statement, classifies payor, or marks as non-commission.
RESULTClose locked · resolution required · rule learned for next time
4Non-Commission Flows

Not every deposit is commission. CASEY knows the difference.

Two categories of money that hit the bank but must never be counted as producer commission.

↔

6900 → 8026 sweeps
SKIP
AndDone premium + fees land in 6900 Trust. After wholesaler is paid, net commission sweeps to 8026 Operating. Both the inflow AND the sweep hit the bank. If CASEY counts both, commission is double-counted.
DETECT: matched pair between accounts, same-day or near-day transfer
ACTION: skip both sides · only count the wholesaler-paid commission
$
Kickers & House Revenue
bonuses · profit share
HOUSE
Satellite Q4 bonusCarrier profit shareContingent revenue
These arrive as real money but they're earned by the agency as a whole, not by producers on specific policies. 100% routes to house. No producer split. No commission row in payout.
CLASSIFY: payor_type = 'kicker' in payor_carrier_map
ACTION: income_type = 'bonus' · skip comp engine

7Period Membership

Which month does each row belong to?

Not always the deposit date. CASEY uses different rules for different pipelines so the close stays clean and edits don't retroactively change past payouts.

IV
Ivans rows
Belong to the Ivans statement date, not the bank deposit date. If Ivans says Chubb statement dated 03/28 and bank didn't receive until 04/03, it's still a March row.
RULEperiod = statement_date.month
NI
Non-Ivans rows
Belong to the bank-received date. No statement from Ivans means no statement date to anchor to. If Foremost check deposits 03/10, it's March.
RULEperiod = bank_deposit_date.month
+Δ
Post-close edits
After a month is closed, never retro-edit. Corrections create a period_adjustment row in the next open period. Prior payouts stay immutable.
RULEforward-adjust → next period
8Verification Lenses

Three sources. Different roles. Not equal votes.

The triple-check isn't three democratic verifiers. Each has a specific job and authority over specific fields. Deal Log wins on attribution. Ivans wins on amounts. NowCerts validates end-of-month totals only.

IV
Ivans
carrier-authoritative
PRIMARY
Source of truth for: policy numbers, commission amounts, effective dates. If Chubb says $1,007.70 on Ivans, that's what Chubb paid.
AUTHORITY: amount · policy_number · carrier
NOT TRUSTED FOR: producer attribution (hints only)
DL
Deal Log
the king
SOURCE
Source of truth for: writing producer, lead source, cross-case assignment, package grouping. Austin's team enters this at close, so it's the most accurate attribution layer.
AUTHORITY: producer · lead_source · package
WINS TIES AGAINST: Ivans writer hints, NowCerts records
NC
NowCerts
validation only
AUDIT
End-of-month validation. Processes Ivans data through a separate AMS pipeline to catch parse errors, missing policies, or total mismatches. Never used for producer attribution.
AUTHORITY: sanity check on totals
NOT TRUSTED FOR: producer attribution (data quality known to drift)

10Close Blockers

The close button is locked until these conditions clear.

No producer gets paid while any of these exist in the period. Close is an event, not a button you hit whenever. CASEY enforces the rules.

!
Conditions that block close
All must be zero before Gusto CSV generates
01Unexplained bank deposits
Any deposit CASEY can't attribute to a pipeline. Orphan money with no known source.
02Unassigned policies
Commission exists but Deal Log has no writer. Austin must assign before payout.
03Unclassified payors
Money arrived from a payor CASEY doesn't know. Must be classified (commission / kicker / rebate / non-comm).
04Bank variance over threshold
Sum of explained commissions off from bank total by more than $500. Needs investigation before close.
05Open audit disputes
Any triple-check conflict (producer, lead source, etc.) not yet resolved by Austin.
