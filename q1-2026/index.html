Technology

Meet CASEY

We built CASEY this quarter. From zero to a fully operational AI assistant integrated into our systems. Here's everything it can do.

CASEY is Safeshield's AI assistant. It runs on Anthropic's Sonnet model, works as an app on your phone, and is connected directly to our management system, email, calendar, and deal log. It was built entirely in Q1 2026.

💬
Insurance Q&A: Ask any coverage, carrier, or risk placement question and get an instant answer
🏢
69 Carriers: Knows every carrier we represent, what they write, where, and what they're competitive on
📧
Gmail Integration: Read, search, and draft emails without leaving the app
📅
Calendar Access: Check your schedule, create events, send invites
📊
Deal Logging: Log every deal directly to the Safeshield Log with one message
📰
Industry News: Latest headlines from Insurance Journal, always up to date
📱
PWA: Works like a native app on your phone. No app store needed.
🔗
NowCerts Integration: Full read/write access with 17 tools. Look up clients, create records, add vehicles, drivers, properties, notes, and tasks.
⚡
One-Sentence Lead Intake: Describe a lead in plain English. CASEY parses every detail and builds the full client file automatically.
🃏
Confirmation Cards: Review everything CASEY parsed before it creates anything. One click to confirm, one click to cancel.
📄
PDF Dec Page Reading: Upload a dec page or quote PDF. CASEY extracts all the data and builds the record from it.
🔎
Client Lookup: Pull up any client's full profile, contact info, policies, and activity history instantly.
🛡️
Duplicate Detection: Searches NowCerts before creating any record. No accidental duplicates.
