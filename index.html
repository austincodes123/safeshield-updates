02 Master SOP

A visual onboarding guide, built to last.

For Marshelene Beukes and every future hire. Live at /sop.html — Safeshield dark theme, org chart, flowcharts, sticky nav.

New · /sop.html

What's inside the SOP

New public SOP page with the full Safeshield dark theme, org chart, flowcharts, and sticky nav. The morning routine is documented end-to-end: NordVPN → Dashlane → Lightspeed → CASEY → NowCerts → Gmail.

Two intake paths documented: Call / Email and Canopy Connect (with the manual backup). Option A / Option B workflow cards for every scenario.

Team roster is current: Austin (CEO), Eric (VP), Alyssa (Commercial), Marshelene (PL). Jerry Abramson, Lisa, Justin, Fortis Lux, and Erie have been removed. PL re-shopping section replaces the old commission / comp section.

Full CASEY section — what she is, what she does, her automations, and how to use her. Callout on missing downloads reminds staff that not every carrier uses Ivans — when in doubt, ask Alyssa.

PDF export script prints a clean, offline-friendly version for printed onboarding. SAFESHIELD_MASTER_SOP.md lives in the repo as the source-of-truth markdown.

New SOP sections added

Claims

Route to the carrier, but make the client feel supported the whole way. Always log a Momentum note.

Policy Changes

Log into the carrier portal, make the change, and add a Momentum note with what changed and why.

Cancellations

Always transfer to Austin or Eric first. We try to save the account before processing anything.

Mobile-friendly

Morning routine stacks vertically, org chart cards stack, data tables scroll horizontally, bigger nav tap targets.
