Every weekday · 9:30 AM

One card. Everything coming due.

CASEY drops a single tidy card into the Renewals space, grouped into windows so it’s never an endless scroll. Within 30 days is the most urgent, so start there. 30-45 and 45-60 days come up behind it. Missing renewal amount means the new premium isn’t in NowCerts yet and needs a re-shop. Completed are the ones already closed out, so they don’t clutter your list. Tap any window to open it.

The renewal detail

Everything in one place

No more bouncing around NowCerts. Open a renewal and it’s all right there: year-over-year premium and the percent change, the household’s other policies with us, how long they’ve been a client, and the full history of who touched it and what they did. Work it from the popup

Four buttons, the whole workflow

Working

I’m on it. Marks it in progress and sets a 3-day follow-up. It drops off your list until then, then comes back so it never gets forgotten. Tap it the moment you start.

Add note

Jot what you did. “Shopped with Plymouth Rock, waiting on quote.” “Emailed the client the new premium.” It saves to NowCerts and to the history.

Looks good ✓

Closing it out. Pick the outcome (Looks good, Renewed, Switched carrier, Lost, Other), add a note if you want, and it drops off your list.

Snooze

Not ready yet? Hide it for 1 day, 3 days, or a week, and it resurfaces when it’s time.

Behind the scenes

She keeps everyone honest, gently

CASEY watches the pipeline so nothing slips, without nagging anyone who’s already on it.

☀️ Midday flag

Around noon each weekday she flags anything slipping: renewals 15 days out and untouched, missing premiums, and big jumps of 25% or more. Austin and Eric get the heads-up. She skips whatever’s already being worked.

🏆 Friday recap

Friday afternoon she posts a recap of everything the team worked that week, by person. A clean end-of-week scoreboard.

It remembers, even after you close the tab. Everything you click sticks. Closed-out renewals stay closed. Working and snoozed items stay hidden until their follow-up date, then come back. Nothing re-appears the next morning or after an update. CASEY picks up exactly where you left off.
